KAT Data Labs

At KAT Data Labs, we build solutions to advance human understanding using data science, artificial intelligence and data visualization.
 
We were founded in 2020 when we collaborated with physicians on several research related projects and helped with an assisted diagnosis tool, a computer vision based analysis tool and a data science and visualization based analysis of image data.
 
Our experience is in programming and automation but our team has broad scientific background and communication skills to connect with doctors and scientists to solve real problems with data.
